Lord of War
The mood of Lord of War is cynical, clever, and biting.
The plot centers around arms dealing, a rise and fall, and human nature.
It is an independent, thriller, and crime movie.
Stylistically, Lord of War includes a voice over.
In approach, it is realistic.
The setting is Europe.
Lord of War happens in the 1980s and in the 1990s.
Note that it includes strong violent content, drugs/alcohol, and nudity.
