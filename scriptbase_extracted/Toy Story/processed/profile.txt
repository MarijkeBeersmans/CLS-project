Toy Story
The mood of Toy Story is witty, feel good, and stylized.
The plot centers around an odd couple, a daring rescue, and imaginary themes.
It is an animation, adventure, and family movie.
Stylistically, Toy Story has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is fantastical.
The setting is the USA.
Toy Story happens in the 1990s.
Visually, it is computer animated.
The movie is known for being groundbreaking, a modern classic, and a blockbuster.
Toy Story is especially suggested for a family outing.
