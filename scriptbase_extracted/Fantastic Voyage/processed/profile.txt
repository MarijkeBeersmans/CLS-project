Fantastic Voyage
Fantastic Voyage can be described as stylized, exciting, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around space travel, an experiment gone awry, and a master villain.
The main genres are adventure, sci-fi, and thriller.
In approach, Fantastic Voyage is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a submarine.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
Fantastic Voyage is well suited for a boys' night.
