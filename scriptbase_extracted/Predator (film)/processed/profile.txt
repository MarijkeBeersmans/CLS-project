Predator
Predator can be described as scary, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around evil aliens, deadly creatures, and survival.
The main genres are action, horror, and sci-fi.
In terms of style, Predator is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a jungle.
Predator takes place in the 1980s.
Visually, it involves special effects.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Predator is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves strong violent content and profanity.
