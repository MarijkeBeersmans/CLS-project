Life as a House
The mood of Life as a House is bittersweet, emotional, and sincere.
The plot centers around a midlife crisis, a dysfunctional family, and themes of unfulfillment.
It is a drama movie.
Stylistically, Life as a House is a melodrama, is talky, and is gothic.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The pacing is slow.
Life as a House takes place, at least partly, in the suburbs.
The setting is California.
It happens in contemporary times.
Note that Life as a House includes sexual content.
