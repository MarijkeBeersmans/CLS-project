American Beauty
The mood of American Beauty is biting, cynical, and clever.
The plot centers around a humdrum life, suburban life, and a midlife crisis.
It features satire.
American Beauty is a drama movie.
Stylistically, it includes a voice over.
In approach, it is realistic.
American Beauty takes place, at least partly, in the suburbs.
The setting is the USA.
It happens in the 1990s.
American Beauty is known for being a modern classic, an Oscar winner, and essential viewing.
Note that it includes brief nudity, mild violent content, and drugs/alcohol.
