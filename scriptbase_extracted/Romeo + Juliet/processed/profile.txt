Romeo + Juliet
Romeo + Juliet can be described as stylized, atmospheric, and clever.
The plot revolves around true love, tragic love, and young lovers.
The main genres are romance and drama.
In approach, Romeo + Juliet is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in the suburbs.
It is located in California.
Romeo + Juliet takes place in the 1990s.
It is adapted from a play.
The movie has received attention for being a Berlin festival winner and critically acclaimed.
Romeo + Juliet is well suited for a date night and teens.
