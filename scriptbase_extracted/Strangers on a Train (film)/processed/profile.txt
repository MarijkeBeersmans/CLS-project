Strangers on a Train
Strangers on a Train can be described as cynical, clever, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around psychological motives, murder, and dishonesty.
The main genres are thriller, crime, and is a film noir.
In approach, Strangers on a Train is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, on a train.
It is located in the USA.
Strangers on a Train takes place in the 1950s.
Visually, it is black and white.
It is based on a book.
Strangers on a Train has received attention for being a masterpiece, a classic, and critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves violent content.
