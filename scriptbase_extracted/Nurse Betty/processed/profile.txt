Nurse Betty
Nurse Betty can be described as cynical, clever, and witty.
The plot revolves around a losing-it hero, memory loss, and an obsessive quest.
The main genres are drama, comedy, and independent.
In approach, Nurse Betty is realistic.
It is located in Los Angeles.
It takes place in the 1990s.
Nurse Betty has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Note that it involves profanity.
