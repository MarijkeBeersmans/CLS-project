Hollow Man
Hollow Man can be described as scary, rough, and stylized.
The plot revolves around a mad scientist, superhuman powers, and an experiment gone awry.
The main genres are horror, thriller, and sci-fi.
In terms of style, Hollow Man has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It takes place in the 1990s.
Hollow Man has received attention for being an award winner.
It is well suited for teens and a boys' night.
Note that it involves strong violent content.
