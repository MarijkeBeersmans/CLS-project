The French Connection
The French Connection can be described as tense, captivating, and rough.
The plot revolves around a tough law enforcer, partners against crime, and drug dealing.
The main genres are thriller, action, and crime.
In approach, The French Connection is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
It is located in New York.
The French Connection takes place in the 20th century.
It is originally a true story and based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being groundbreaking, a masterpiece, and a classic.
The French Connection is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol, profanity, and violent content.
