Prometheus
Prometheus can be described as tense, stylized, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around an alien encounter, androids and robots, and an obsessive quest.
The main genres are sci-fi, thriller, and horror.
In terms of style, Prometheus is futuristic and has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, on a distant planet and on a spacecraft.
Visually, Prometheus is 3D and involves special effects.
The movie has received attention for being a blockbuster.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
