Man on the Moon
The mood of Man on the Moon is offbeat, touching, and bittersweet.
The plot centers around terminal illness, celebrity culture, and the life of a performer.
It is a drama and comedy movie.
In approach, Man on the Moon is realistic.
The setting is New York.
It happens in the 1980s.
The musical score of Man on the Moon is alternative, pop, and rock.
The movie is known for being a Berlin festival winner.
Note that it includes sexual content and profanity.
