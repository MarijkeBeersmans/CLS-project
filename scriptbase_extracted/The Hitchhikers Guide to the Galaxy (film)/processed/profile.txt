The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy
The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy can be described as witty, stylized, and offbeat.
The plot revolves around space travel, space and aliens, and misfits.
Its comic aspect comes from silly humor.
Its main genres are comedy, adventure, and sci-fi.
In approach, it is not serious and fantastical.
It takes place in the future.
It is based on a book.
Note that it involves mild violent content.
