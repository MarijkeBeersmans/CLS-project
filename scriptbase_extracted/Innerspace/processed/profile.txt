Innerspace
Innerspace can be described as captivating, humorous, and touching.
The plot revolves around an experiment gone awry, a race against time, and disorder.
The main genres are comedy, sci-fi, and adventure.
In approach, Innerspace is fantastical.
It takes place in the 1980s.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an Oscar winner.
Innerspace is well suited for teens.
Note that it involves mild violent content and profanity.
