Kafka
Kafka can be described as atmospheric, suspenseful, and stylized.
The plot revolves around a missing person, uncovering the truth, and an investigation.
The main genres are drama, thriller, and is expressionist.
In terms of style, Kafka is surreal.
In approach, it is serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a palace, castle, or temple.
Kafka takes place in the 1910s.
