Domino
Domino can be described as rough, stylized, and offbeat.
The plot revolves around bounty hunters, criminal heroes, and way of life.
The main genres are action and crime.
In terms of style, Domino is postmodernist, is nonlinear, and includes a voice over.
In approach, it is realistic.
It is located in Los Angeles.
Domino is originally a true story.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves strong sexual content, drugs/alcohol, and nudity.
