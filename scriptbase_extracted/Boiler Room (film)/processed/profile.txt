Boiler Room
Boiler Room can be described as cynical, clever, and captivating.
The plot revolves around a moral dilemma, a rise to the top, and cons and scams.
The main genres are drama and crime.
In approach, Boiler Room is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an office.
It is located in New York.
Boiler Room takes place in the 1990s.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol.
