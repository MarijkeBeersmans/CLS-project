The Faculty
The mood of The Faculty is scary and suspenseful.
The plot centers around evil aliens, teachers and students, and power relations.
It is a horror and sci-fi movie.
In approach, The Faculty is fantastical and serious.
It takes place, at least partly, at a high school and in a small town.
It happens in the 1990s.
The Faculty is especially suggested for teens.
Note that it includes brief nudity, drugs/alcohol, and violent content.
