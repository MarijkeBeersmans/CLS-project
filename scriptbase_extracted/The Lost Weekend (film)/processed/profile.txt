The Lost Weekend
The Lost Weekend can be described as bleak, gloomy, and captivating.
The plot revolves around alcohol abuse, themes of life is a bitch, and themes of mind and soul.
The main genre is drama.
In approach, The Lost Weekend is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
It is located in New York.
The Lost Weekend takes place in the 20th century.
Visually, it is black and white.
It is based on a book.
The Lost Weekend has received attention for being a classic, a Cannes festival winner, and a Golden Globe winner.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol.
