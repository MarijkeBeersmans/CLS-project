Alien
The mood of Alien is tense, captivating, and scary.
The plot centers around evil aliens, space travel, and androids and robots.
It is a thriller, horror, and sci-fi movie.
Stylistically, Alien has a Hollywood tone, stars a strong female character, and stars an ensemble cast.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
The pacing is slow.
Alien takes place, at least partly, on a spacecraft.
It happens in the future.
Visually, it involves special effects.
Alien is known for being a classic, a blockbuster, and critically acclaimed.
