Croupier
Croupier can be described as clever, contemplative, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around gambling, human nature, and power relations.
The main genre is crime.
In approach, Croupier is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
It is set, at least in part, in a casino.
Croupier is located in London.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
Note that Croupier involves mild violent content and profanity.
