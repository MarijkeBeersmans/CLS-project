Barry Lyndon
Barry Lyndon can be described as cynical, captivating, and clever.
The plot revolves around a rise and fall, self discovery, and power relations.
The main genres are drama and period.
In terms of style, Barry Lyndon is epic and includes a voice over.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Barry Lyndon is located in Ireland and England.
It takes place in the 18th century.
It is based on a book.
Barry Lyndon has received attention for being a masterpiece, essential viewing, and critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves brief nudity and mild violent content.
