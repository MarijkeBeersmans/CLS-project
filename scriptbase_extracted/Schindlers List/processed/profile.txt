Schindler's List
Schindler's List can be described as disturbing, bleak, and emotional.
The plot revolves around a conspiracy against humanity, antiheroes, and a rescue.
The main genres are drama, historical, and war.
In terms of style, Schindler's List is epic.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a ghetto and in a concentration camp.
Schindler's List is located in Europe.
It takes place in the 20th century.
Visually, it is partly black and white.
Schindler's List is originally a true story.
The movie has received attention for being a modern classic, a masterpiece, and an Oscar winner.
Note that it involves nudity and profanity.
