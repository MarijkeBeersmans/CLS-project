Beasts of the Southern Wild
Beasts of the Southern Wild can be described as stylized, atmospheric, and offbeat.
The plot revolves around kids in trouble, being down on your luck, and social relations.
The main genres are drama and independent.
In terms of style, Beasts of the Southern Wild is surreal and includes a voice over.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in Louisiana.
Beasts of the Southern Wild takes place in contemporary times.
It is adapted from a play.
The movie has received attention for being a Sundance Festival winner, a Cannes festival winner, and critically acclaimed.
Note that Beasts of the Southern Wild involves drugs/alcohol and profanity.
