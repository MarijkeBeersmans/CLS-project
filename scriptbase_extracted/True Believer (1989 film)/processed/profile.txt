True Believer
True Believer can be described as clever, biting, and captivating.
The plot revolves around legal action, righting wrongs, and legal issues.
The main genres are drama, thriller, and crime.
In terms of style, True Believer is neo-noir and involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in the USA.
True Believer takes place in the 1980s.
Note that it involves profanity and violent content.
