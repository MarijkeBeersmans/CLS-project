Midnight Express
The mood of Midnight Express is captivating, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot centers around a prison breakout, prison life, and injustice.
It is a drama, independent, and thriller movie.
In approach, Midnight Express is serious and realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in a prison.
The setting is Turkey.
Midnight Express happens in the 20th century.
It is originally a true story and based on a book.
The movie is known for being a classic, an Oscar winner, and critically acclaimed.
Note that Midnight Express includes brief nudity and strong violent content.
