Hard Rain
Hard Rain can be described as suspenseful.
The plot revolves around witnessing a crime, forces of nature, and a treasure hunt.
The main genres are thriller, action, and crime.
In approach, Hard Rain is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is fast paced.
It is set, at least in part, in a small town.
Hard Rain takes place in the 1990s.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves mild violent content.
