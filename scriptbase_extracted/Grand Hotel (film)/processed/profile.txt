Grand Hotel
Grand Hotel can be described as bittersweet, stylized, and sentimental.
The plot revolves around human nature, falling in love, and couples relations.
The main genres are drama and romance.
In terms of style, Grand Hotel stars an ensemble cast and is a melodrama.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Grand Hotel is set, at least in part, in a hotel.
It is located in Germany.
Visually, it is black and white.
Grand Hotel is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being an Oscar winner.
