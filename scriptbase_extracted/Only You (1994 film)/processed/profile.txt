Only You
Only You can be described as sentimental, feel good, and touching.
The plot revolves around teaching the ways of the world, true love, and unfulfilled love.
The main genres are comedy and romance.
In approach, Only You is realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an airport.
It is located in Venice.
Only You takes place in the 1990s.
It is well suited for a girls' night and a date night.
