Dog Day Afternoon
Dog Day Afternoon can be described as tense, gloomy, and captivating.
The plot revolves around being transsexual, hostages, and crime gone awry.
The main genres are drama and crime.
In approach, Dog Day Afternoon is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a bank.
It is located in New York.
Dog Day Afternoon takes place in the 20th century.
It is inspired by an article and originally a true story.
The movie has received attention for being a masterpiece, a classic, and an Oscar winner.
Note that Dog Day Afternoon involves violent content.
