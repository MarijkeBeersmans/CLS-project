The Sweet Hereafter
The Sweet Hereafter can be described as contemplative, emotional, and bleak.
The plot revolves around a hotshot lawyer, dealing with death, and social relations.
The main genres are drama and independent.
In terms of style, The Sweet Hereafter is nonlinear, involves multiple stories, and is a melodrama.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
The Sweet Hereafter is set, at least in part, on a bus and in a small town.
It is located in Canada.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The Sweet Hereafter is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being a masterpiece, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
Note that it involves sexual content.
