Psycho
The mood of Psycho is tense, atmospheric, and scary.
The plot centers around a character with a split personality, a woman in danger, and psychological motives.
It is an independent, thriller, and horror movie.
Stylistically, Psycho includes a voice over.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in a hotel.
Psycho is set in Arizona.
It happens in the 1950s.
Visually, it is black and white.
The musical score of Psycho is orchestral.
It is based on a book.
The movie is known for being a masterpiece, a classic, and essential viewing.
Note that Psycho includes brief nudity and violent content.
