U-Turn
U-Turn can be described as disturbing, suspenseful, and sexual.
The plot revolves around an assassination, betrayal, and dishonesty.
The main genres are thriller and crime.
In terms of style, U-Turn features an all-star cast and is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
U-Turn is set, at least in part, in a small town.
It is located in Arizona.
It takes place in the 1990s.
U-Turn is based on a book.
Note that it involves violent content.
