Pretty Woman
Pretty Woman can be described as feel good and sexy.
The plot revolves around a Cinderella story, opposites attracting, and social differences.
The main genres are comedy and romance.
In terms of style, Pretty Woman has a Hollywood tone and is fairytale-like.
In approach, it is realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
Pretty Woman is located in Beverly Hills.
It takes place in the 1980s.
The soundtrack is pop.
Pretty Woman has received attention for being a modern classic, a blockbuster, and an award winner.
It is well suited for a girls' night and a date night.
Note that it involves sexual content and profanity.
