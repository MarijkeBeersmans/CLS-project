El Mariachi
El Mariachi can be described as exciting, suspenseful, and stylized.
The plot revolves around unlikely criminals, a violence spree, and mistaken identities.
The main genres are independent, action, and crime.
In approach, El Mariachi is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a small town.
It is located in Mexico.
El Mariachi takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being a Sundance Festival winner and critically acclaimed.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
