500 Days of Summer
500 Days of Summer can be described as witty, bittersweet, and clever.
The plot revolves around a breakup, obsession, and themes of unfulfillment.
The main genres are independent, comedy, and romance.
In terms of style, 500 Days of Summer is nonlinear, includes a voice over, and is talky.
In approach, it is realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
500 Days of Summer is located in Los Angeles.
It takes place in contemporary times.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
500 Days of Summer is well suited for a date night.
Note that it involves profanity.
