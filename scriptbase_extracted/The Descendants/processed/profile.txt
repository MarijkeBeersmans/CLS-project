The Descendants
The Descendants can be described as touching, bittersweet, and clever.
The plot revolves around family problems, parents and children, and family relations.
The main genres are drama and independent.
In terms of style, The Descendants has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is realistic.
It is located in Hawaii.
The Descendants takes place in contemporary times.
It is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being an Oscar winner and critically acclaimed.
Note that The Descendants involves profanity.
