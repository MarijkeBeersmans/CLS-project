Resident Evil
Resident Evil can be described as scary, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around an experiment gone awry, zombies, and survival.
The main genres are independent, thriller, and action.
In terms of style, Resident Evil is futuristic, stars a strong female character, and is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
Visually, it involves special effects.
Resident Evil is derived from a video game.
It is well suited for teens and a boys' night.
Note that it involves brief nudity, profanity, and violent content.
