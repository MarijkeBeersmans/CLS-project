Body Heat
Body Heat can be described as cynical, sexual, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around the protagonist as suspect, a dangerous attraction, and betrayal.
The main genres are thriller and crime.
In terms of style, Body Heat is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a small town.
Body Heat is located in Florida.
It takes place in the 1980s.
Note that it involves nudity and violent content.
