The Cooler
The Cooler can be described as bittersweet, gloomy, and sexy.
The plot revolves around gambling, being down on your luck, and a dangerous attraction.
The main genres are drama, independent, and romance.
In approach, The Cooler is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
It is set, at least in part, in a casino.
The Cooler is located in Las Vegas.
It takes place in contemporary times.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Note that The Cooler involves strong sexual content, nudity, and profanity.
