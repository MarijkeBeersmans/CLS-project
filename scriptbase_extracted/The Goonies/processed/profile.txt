The Goonies
The mood of The Goonies is feel good and sentimental.
The plot centers around a treasure hunt, mischievous children, and young heroes.
It is an adventure, family, and comedy movie.
Stylistically, The Goonies has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in a small town.
The Goonies is set in the USA.
It happens in the 1980s.
The musical score is pop.
The Goonies is known for being a blockbuster and an award winner.
It is especially suggested for a family outing.
Note that it includes mild violent content.
