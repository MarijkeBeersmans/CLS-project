The Seventh Victim
The Seventh Victim can be described as scary, suspenseful, and mind bending.
The plot revolves around a missing person, religions or cults, and danger.
The main genres are horror, thriller, and mystery.
In terms of style, The Seventh Victim is gothic.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
The Seventh Victim is located in New York.
Visually, it is black and white.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
