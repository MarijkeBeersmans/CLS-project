All About Eve
All About Eve can be described as cynical, clever, and witty.
The plot revolves around aging, showbiz, and ambition.
The main genres are drama and comedy.
In terms of style, All About Eve has a Hollywood tone and is talky.
In approach, it is realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
All About Eve is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
It is located in New York.
It takes place in the 1950s.
Visually, All About Eve is black and white.
It is inspired by an article, derived from a short story, and originally a true story.
The movie has received attention for being a classic, an Oscar winner, and critically acclaimed.
