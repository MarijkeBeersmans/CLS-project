Rapture
The plot of Rapture revolves around fugitives, opposites attracting, and falling in love.
The main genres are drama and independent.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
Rapture is set, at least in part, on a farm.
It is located in France.
It takes place in the 1960s.
Visually, Rapture is black and white.
It is based on a book.
