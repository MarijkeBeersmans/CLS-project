Blue Velvet
Blue Velvet can be described as disturbing, sexual, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around suburban life, Americana, and a woman in danger.
The main genres are drama, independent, and mystery.
In terms of style, Blue Velvet is postmodernist, is neo-noir, and is surreal.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in the suburbs and in a small town.
Blue Velvet takes place in the 1980s.
The movie has received attention for being original, a cult favorite, and critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves strong sexual content, drugs/alcohol, and nudity.
