Jennifer's Body
Jennifer's Body can be described as sexy, sexual, and offbeat.
The plot revolves around high school life, curses, spells and rituals, and social relations.
Its comic aspect comes from dark humor.
Jennifer's Body's main genres are horror, comedy, and thriller.
In approach, it is fantastical.
It is set, at least in part, at a high school and in a small town.
Jennifer's Body is well suited for teens.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol, nudity, and sexual content.
