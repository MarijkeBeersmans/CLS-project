Lone Star
Lone Star can be described as tense, mind bending, and clever.
The plot revolves around uncovering the truth, an investigation, and murder.
The main genres are drama, independent, and mystery.
In terms of style, Lone Star involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Lone Star is set, at least in part, in a small town.
It is located in Texas.
It takes place in the 1990s.
Lone Star has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Note that it involves mild violent content.
