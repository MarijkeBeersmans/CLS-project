Superman: The Movie
Superman: The Movie can be described as exciting, feel good, and suspenseful.

The main genres are adventure, fantasy, and action.

In approach, it is fantastical and serious.

Visually, Superman: The Movie involves special effects.
It is adapted from a comic.
The movie has received attention for being a classic, essential viewing, and a blockbuster.
Superman: The Movie is well suited for a family outing and teens.
