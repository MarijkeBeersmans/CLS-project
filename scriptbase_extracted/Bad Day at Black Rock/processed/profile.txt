Bad Day at Black Rock
The mood of Bad Day at Black Rock is tense, atmospheric, and suspenseful.
The plot centers around crimes, a missing person, and small town life.
It is a drama, thriller, and western movie.
In approach, Bad Day at Black Rock is serious and realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in a small town.
It happens in the 20th century.
Bad Day at Black Rock is based on a book.
The movie is known for being a masterpiece, a classic, and a Cannes festival winner.
Note that it includes violent content.
