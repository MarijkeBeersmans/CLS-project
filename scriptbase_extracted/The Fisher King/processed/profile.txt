The Fisher King
The mood of The Fisher King is bittersweet, captivating, and touching.
The plot centers around redemption, self discovery, and being haunted by the past.
It is a drama movie.
Stylistically, The Fisher King is surreal.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in an urban environment.
The Fisher King is set in New York.
It happens in the 1990s.
The movie is known for being critically acclaimed and an Oscar winner.
Note that The Fisher King includes drugs/alcohol, nudity, and profanity.
