Alone in the Dark
Alone in the Dark can be described as scary, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around an obsessive quest, curses, spells and rituals, and a race against time.
The main genres are horror, thriller, and action.
In terms of style, Alone in the Dark involves twists and turns and is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It takes place in ancient times.
Alone in the Dark is derived from a video game.
It is well suited for teens and a boys' night.
Note that it involves profanity.
