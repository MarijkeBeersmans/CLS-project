Event Horizon
The mood of Event Horizon is bleak, scary, and suspenseful.
The plot centers around space travel, a future dystopia, and ghosts.
It is a thriller, horror, and sci-fi movie.
Stylistically, Event Horizon is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is especially suggested for a boys' night.
