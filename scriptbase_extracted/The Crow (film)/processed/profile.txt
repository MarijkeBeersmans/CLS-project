The Crow
The Crow can be described as stylized, rough, and exciting.
The plot revolves around the afterlife, a master criminal, and superheroes.
The main genres are thriller, action, and fantasy.
In terms of style, The Crow is gothic and includes a voice over.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
The Crow takes place in the 1990s.
The soundtrack is alternative and rock.
It is adapted from a comic.
The Crow has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol, nudity, and profanity.
