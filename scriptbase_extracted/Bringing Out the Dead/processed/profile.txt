Bringing Out the Dead
The mood of Bringing Out the Dead is bleak, captivating, and suspenseful.
The plot centers around dealing with death, redemption, and psychological motives.
It is a drama movie.

The setting is New York.
It happens in the 1990s.
Bringing Out the Dead is based on a book.
The movie is known for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
