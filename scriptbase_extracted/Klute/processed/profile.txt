Klute
Klute can be described as gloomy, captivating, and cynical.
The plot revolves around a prostitute, a private detective, and crimes.
The main genres are thriller, mystery, and drama.
In terms of style, Klute is neo-noir, is talky, and stars a strong female character.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
Klute is located in New York.
It takes place in the 20th century.
The movie has received attention for being an Oscar winner and critically acclaimed.
