Fargo
The mood of Fargo is cynical, witty, and clever.
The plot centers around Americana, kidnapping, and a psychopath.
It features dark humor.
Fargo is an independent, crime, and comedy movie.
Stylistically, it is neo-noir and is gory.
In approach, it is realistic.
Fargo takes place, at least partly, in a small town.
The setting is Minnesota.
It happens in the 1980s.
Fargo is known for being an Oscar winner, essential viewing, and critically acclaimed.
