Pet Sematary II
Pet Sematary II can be described as scary, suspenseful, and tense.
The plot revolves around vengeance, supernatural occurences, and deadly occurences.
The main genre is horror.
In terms of style, Pet Sematary II is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a small town.
Pet Sematary II is located in Maine.
It takes place in the 1990s.
It is well suited for teens.
Note that Pet Sematary II involves brief nudity, strong violent content, and sexual content.
