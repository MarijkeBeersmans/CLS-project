The Perks of Being a Wallflower
The Perks of Being a Wallflower can be described as bittersweet, touching, and gloomy.
The plot revolves around social misfits, coming of age, and teenage life.
The main genre is drama.
In terms of style, it includes a voice over.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in the suburbs and at a high school.
It is located in Pennsylvania.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The soundtrack is alternative, pop, and rock.
It is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol and sexual content.
