American Pie
American Pie can be described as sexual and humorous.
The plot revolves around losing virginity, looking for sex, and teenage love.
Its comic aspect comes from grossout humor and silly humor.
American Pie's main genre is comedy.
In terms of style, it has a Hollywood tone and stars an ensemble cast.
In approach, it is not serious and realistic.
American Pie is set, at least in part, in the suburbs.
It is located in Michigan.
It takes place in the 1990s.
American Pie has received attention for being a modern classic, a blockbuster, and an award winner.
It is well suited for a date night and teens.
Note that it involves strong sexual content, nudity, and profanity.
