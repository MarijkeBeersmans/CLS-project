The Shining
The mood of The Shining is bleak, tense, and atmospheric.
The plot centers around a losing-it hero, isolation, and ghosts.
It is a horror movie.
Stylistically, The Shining is surreal.
In approach, it is serious.
It takes place, at least partly, indoors and in a hotel.
The Shining is set in Colorado.
It happens in the 20th century.
It is based on a book.
The Shining is known for being a modern classic, a masterpiece, and essential viewing.
