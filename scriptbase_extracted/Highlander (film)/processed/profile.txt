Highlander
Highlander can be described as sincere, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a tragic hero, lone wolves, and supernatural abilities.
The main genres are action, sci-fi, and fantasy.
In approach, Highlander is fantastical and serious.
It is located in Scotland and New York.
It takes place in the 16th century and in the 1980s.
Highlander has received attention for being a cult favorite.
It is well suited for teens.
