Who Framed Roger Rabbit?
Who Framed Roger Rabbit? can be described as feel good, exciting, and stylized.
The plot revolves around a parallel world, a frame-up, and an odd couple.
Its comic aspect comes from satire.
Who Framed Roger Rabbit?'s main genres are comedy, fantasy, and animation.
In terms of style, it is neo-noir and has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is not serious and fantastical.
Who Framed Roger Rabbit? is located in Hollywood.
It takes place in the 20th century.
Visually, it includes animation and live action.
Who Framed Roger Rabbit? is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being groundbreaking, original, and a modern classic.
It is well suited for a date night, a family outing, and teens.
