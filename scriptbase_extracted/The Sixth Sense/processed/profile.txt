The Sixth Sense
The Sixth Sense can be described as mind bending, bleak, and gloomy.
The plot revolves around doctors and patients, ghosts, and supernatural abilities.
The main genres are drama, mystery, and thriller.
In terms of style, The Sixth Sense has a Hollywood tone and involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is serious.
It is located in Philadelphia.
The Sixth Sense takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being original, a modern classic, and a blockbuster.
Note that it involves mild violent content.
