Hackers
Hackers can be described as suspenseful, tense, and exciting.
The plot revolves around computers and the web, a frame-up, and mind games.
The main genres are thriller and crime.
In approach, Hackers is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, at a high school.
It is located in New York.
Hackers takes place in the 1990s.
It is well suited for teens.
Note that it involves violent content.
