Mirrors
Mirrors can be described as tense, scary, and rough.
The plot revolves around a family in danger, uncovering the truth, and ghosts.
The main genres are horror and thriller.
In terms of style, Mirrors involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a store.
Mirrors takes place in contemporary times.
It is inspired by a film and a remake.
Note that it involves brief nudity and profanity.
