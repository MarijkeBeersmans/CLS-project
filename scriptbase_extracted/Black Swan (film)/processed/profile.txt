Black Swan
The mood of Black Swan is mind bending, stylized, and sexual.
The plot centers around a losing-it hero, dancers, and the life of a performer.
It is a drama and independent movie.
Stylistically, Black Swan is surreal.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The pacing is slow.
Black Swan is set in New York.
It happens in contemporary times.
The movie is known for being an Oscar winner, a blockbuster, and critically acclaimed.
Note that Black Swan includes strong sexual content, drugs/alcohol, and profanity.
