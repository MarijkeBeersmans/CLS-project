The Thin Man
The Thin Man can be described as clever, witty, and feel good.
The plot revolves around a private detective, a whodunit, and couples relations.
The main genres are is a film noir, comedy, and crime.
In approach, The Thin Man is realistic.
It is located in New York.
It takes place in the 1930s.
Visually, The Thin Man is black and white.
It is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
Note that The Thin Man involves mild violent content.
