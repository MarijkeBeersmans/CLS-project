New York Minute
New York Minute can be described as humorous and feel good.
The plot revolves around twins, disorder, and friends.
Its comic aspect comes from farce and silly humor.
New York Minute's main genre is comedy.
In approach, it is not serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
New York Minute is located in New York.
It takes place in contemporary times.
