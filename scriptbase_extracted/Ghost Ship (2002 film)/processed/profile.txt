Ghost Ship
Ghost Ship can be described as scary, suspenseful, and mind bending.
The plot revolves around isolation, ghosts, and supernatural abilities.
The main genre is horror.
In terms of style, Ghost Ship involves twists and turns and is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a haunted house and on a ship.
Ghost Ship is located in Alaska.
It takes place in contemporary times.
It is well suited for teens and a boys' night.
Note that Ghost Ship involves sexual content and violent content.
