Jurassic Park
Jurassic Park can be described as exciting, stylized, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a child in danger, dangerous animals, and an experiment gone awry.
The main genres are adventure, action, and sci-fi.
In terms of style, Jurassic Park has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a theme park and on an island.
Jurassic Park takes place in the 1990s.
Visually, it involves special effects.
The movie has received attention for being a modern classic, a blockbuster, and critically acclaimed.
Jurassic Park is well suited for teens and a boys' night.
Note that it involves violent content.
