My Own Private Idaho
The mood of My Own Private Idaho is atmospheric, captivating, and touching.
The plot centers around gender, partners, and friendship.
It is a drama and independent movie.
Stylistically, My Own Private Idaho is surreal and has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The setting is the USA.
My Own Private Idaho happens in the 1990s.
It is adapted from a play.
The movie is known for being a Venice Festival winner and critically acclaimed.
Note that My Own Private Idaho includes drugs/alcohol.
