Kalifornia
Kalifornia can be described as disturbing, bleak, and tense.
The plot revolves around a murderous pair, lovers on the run, and a violence spree.
The main genres are independent, thriller, and crime.
In terms of style, Kalifornia has a road movie structure, includes a voice over, and is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It takes place in the 1990s.
Kalifornia has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves strong sexual content, strong violent content, and profanity.
