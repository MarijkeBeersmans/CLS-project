Living in Oblivion
The mood of Living in Oblivion is biting, clever, and witty.
The plot centers around an ego clash, the life of an actor, and filmmaking.
It features dark humor and satire.
Living in Oblivion is a drama, comedy, and independent movie.
Stylistically, it is a film in a film, stars an ensemble cast, and is surreal.
It takes place, at least partly, on a movie set.
Living in Oblivion is set in New York.
It happens in the 1990s.
Visually, it is partly black and white.
Living in Oblivion is known for being a Sundance Festival winner and critically acclaimed.
Note that it includes profanity.
