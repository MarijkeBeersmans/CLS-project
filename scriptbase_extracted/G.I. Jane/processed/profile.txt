G.I. Jane
G.I. Jane can be described as sincere, rough, and bleak.
The plot revolves around officers and cadets, basic training, and feminism.
The main genre is drama.
In terms of style, G.I. Jane stars a strong female character.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, on a military base.
G.I. Jane takes place in the 1990s.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves profanity and violent content.
