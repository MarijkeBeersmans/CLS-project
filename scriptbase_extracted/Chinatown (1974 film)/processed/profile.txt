Chinatown
Chinatown can be described as mind bending, cynical, and bleak.
The plot revolves around a private detective, being haunted by the past, and power relations.
The main genres are mystery, drama, and thriller.
In terms of style, Chinatown is neo-noir and involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in Los Angeles.
Chinatown takes place in the 1930s.
The movie has received attention for being a masterpiece, a classic, and an Oscar winner.
Note that it involves violent content.
