The Last Samurai
The mood of The Last Samurai is stylized, captivating, and exciting.
The plot centers around samurai, culture clash, and battles.
It is a war and drama movie.
Stylistically, The Last Samurai has a Hollywood tone and is gory.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The setting is Japan.
The Last Samurai happens in the 19th century.
The movie is known for being a blockbuster and an award winner.
