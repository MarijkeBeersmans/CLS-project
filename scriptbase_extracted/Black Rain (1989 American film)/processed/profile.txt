Black Rain
Black Rain can be described as rough, suspenseful, and stylized.
The plot revolves around a tough law enforcer, partners against crime, and tracking someone down.
The main genres are action and crime.
In terms of style, Black Rain is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in Japan and New York.
Black Rain takes place in the 1980s.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves violent content.
