The Ides of March
The mood of The Ides of March is biting, clever, and thought provoking.
The plot centers around campaign, political intrigue, and politics.
It is a drama movie.
Stylistically, The Ides of March features an all-star cast and is talky.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The setting is Ohio.
The Ides of March happens in contemporary times.
It is adapted from a play and originally a true story.
The movie is known for being a Venice Festival winner and critically acclaimed.
Note that The Ides of March includes profanity.
