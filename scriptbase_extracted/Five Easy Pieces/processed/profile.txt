Five Easy Pieces
Five Easy Pieces can be described as contemplative, sincere, and thought provoking.
The plot revolves around social misfits, a dysfunctional family, and human nature.
The main genre is drama.
In terms of style, Five Easy Pieces has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Five Easy Pieces is set, at least in part, in a small town.
It is located in California.
It takes place in the 20th century.
Five Easy Pieces has received attention for being essential viewing, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
Note that it involves nudity and profanity.
