The Verdict
The Verdict can be described as clever, thought provoking, and contemplative.
The plot revolves around legal action, terminal illness, and righting wrongs.
The main genre is drama.
In terms of style, The Verdict is a melodrama and is talky.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a hospital.
The Verdict is located in Boston.
It takes place in the 1980s.
It is based on a book.
The Verdict has received attention for being a blockbuster and critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves mild violent content and profanity.
