I Know What You Did Last Summer
I Know What You Did Last Summer can be described as scary and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a slasher, a whodunit, and a serial killer.
The main genres are horror, thriller, and mystery.
In terms of style, it involves twists and turns and is gory.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a small town.
It is located in the USA.
It takes place in the 1990s.
It is based on a book.
It is well suited for teens and a date night.
Note that it involves profanity.
