A Perfect World
A Perfect World can be described as contemplative, emotional, and stylized.
The plot revolves around a manhunt, fugitives, and being on the run.
The main genres are drama and crime.
In terms of style, A Perfect World has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in Texas.
A Perfect World takes place in the 1960s.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves profanity and violent content.
