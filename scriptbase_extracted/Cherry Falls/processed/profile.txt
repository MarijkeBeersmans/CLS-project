Cherry Falls
Cherry Falls can be described as scary, suspenseful, and sexual.
The plot revolves around a slasher, a whodunit, and a psychopath.
Its comic aspect comes from satire.
Cherry Falls's main genres are independent and horror.
In terms of style, it is gory.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
Cherry Falls is set, at least in part, in a small town.
It is located in Virginia.
It takes place in the 1990s.
Cherry Falls is well suited for teens.
Note that it involves strong violent content, nudity, and sexual content.
