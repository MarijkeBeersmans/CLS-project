Rear Window
Rear Window can be described as mind bending, clever, and stylized.
The plot revolves around voyeurism, disabilities, and witnessing a crime.
The main genres are thriller, mystery, and crime.
In terms of style, Rear Window involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, indoors and in an urban environment.
Rear Window is located in New York.
It takes place in the 1950s.
It is derived from a short story.
Rear Window has received attention for being a masterpiece, a classic, and essential viewing.
Note that it involves mild violent content.
