The Ugly Truth
The Ugly Truth can be described as feel good and humorous.
The plot revolves around womanizing, teaching the ways of the world, and a battle of the sexes.
The main genres are comedy and romance.
In terms of style, The Ugly Truth has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is realistic.
It takes place in contemporary times.
The Ugly Truth has received attention for being a blockbuster.
It is well suited for a date night.
Note that it involves sexual content and profanity.
