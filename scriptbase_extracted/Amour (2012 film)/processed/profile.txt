Amour
The mood of Amour is gloomy, sincere, and touching.
The plot centers around elderly lovers, the golden years, and couples.
It is a drama and foreign movie.
In approach, Amour is serious and realistic.
The pacing is slow.
It takes place, at least partly, indoors.
Amour is set in Paris.
It happens in contemporary times.
The movie is known for being a Cannes festival winner, a Golden Globe winner, and an Oscar winner.
