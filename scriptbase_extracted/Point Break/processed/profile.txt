Point Break
Point Break can be described as exciting and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around special agents, working undercover, and undercover characters.
The main genres are thriller and action.
In approach, Point Break is serious and realistic.
It is located in Los Angeles.
It takes place in the 1990s.
Point Break is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves brief nudity, sexual content, and profanity.
