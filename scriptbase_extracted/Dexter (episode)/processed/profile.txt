Dexter
Dexter can be described as captivating, suspenseful, and disturbing.
The plot revolves around hidden identities, a serial killer, and misfits.
Its comic aspect comes from dark humor.
Dexter's main genres are thriller and crime.
In terms of style, it includes a voice over and is gory.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
Dexter is set, at least in part, in a police station.
It is located in Miami.
It takes place in contemporary times.
Dexter is based on a book.
The TV show has received attention for being a hit series, a Golden Globe winner, and an Emmy winner.
Note that it involves nudity, sexual content, and profanity.
