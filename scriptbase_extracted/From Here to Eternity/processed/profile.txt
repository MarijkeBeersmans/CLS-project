From Here to Eternity

The plot centers around military life, infidelity, and a love affair.

The pacing is slow.
From Here to Eternity takes place, at least partly, on a military base.
The setting is Hawaii.
It happens in the 20th century.
Visually, From Here to Eternity is black and white.
