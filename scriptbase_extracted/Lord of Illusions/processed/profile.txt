Lord of Illusions
Lord of Illusions can be described as scary, suspenseful, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around wizards and magicians, curses, spells and rituals, and supernatural abilities.
The main genres are thriller, horror, and mystery.
In terms of style, Lord of Illusions is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious.
It is located in Los Angeles and New York.
Lord of Illusions takes place in the 1990s.
