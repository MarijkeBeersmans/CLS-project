The Abyss

The plot centers around an alien encounter, disasters, and a heroic mission.
It is an adventure, sci-fi, and thriller movie.

It takes place, at least partly, in a submarine and in an ocean or sea.

Visually, The Abyss involves special effects.
The movie is known for being critically acclaimed and an Oscar winner.
