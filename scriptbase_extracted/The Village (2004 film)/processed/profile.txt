The Village
The Village can be described as mind bending, gloomy, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around village life, isolation, and uncovering the truth.
The main genres are mystery, drama, and thriller.
In approach, The Village is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a village.
The movie has received attention for being original, a blockbuster, and an award winner.
Note that The Village involves violent content.
