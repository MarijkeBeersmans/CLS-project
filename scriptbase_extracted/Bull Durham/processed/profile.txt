Bull Durham
The mood of Bull Durham is feel good and humorous.
The plot centers around the life of an athlete, self discovery, and ambition.
It is a drama, comedy, and independent movie.
Stylistically, Bull Durham is talky.
In approach, it is realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in a small town.
Bull Durham is set in North Carolina.
It happens in the 1980s.
The movie is known for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Note that Bull Durham includes nudity, sexual content, and profanity.
