The Believer
The Believer can be described as disturbing, thought provoking, and contemplative.
The plot revolves around anti semitism, rebelliousness, and a violence spree.
The main genres are drama, independent, and period.
In approach, The Believer is serious and realistic.
It is located in New York.
It takes place in the 1960s.
The Believer is originally a true story.
The movie has received attention for being a Sundance Festival winner and critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves profanity and violent content.
