Dark City
Dark City can be described as mind bending, stylized, and bleak.
The plot revolves around evil aliens, a future dystopia, and chases.
The main genres are thriller, sci-fi, and mystery.
In terms of style, Dark City is postmodernist and is gothic.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Dark City is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
Visually, it involves special effects.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Note that Dark City involves mild violent content.
