Four Rooms
Four Rooms can be described as humorous, clever, and sexual.
The plot revolves around mischievous children, a "fish out of water", and disorder.
Its comic aspect comes from dark humor.
Four Rooms's main genres are comedy and independent.
In terms of style, it involves multiple stories, is surreal, and stars an ensemble cast.
In approach, it is realistic.
Four Rooms is set, at least in part, in a hotel.
It is located in California.
It takes place in the 1990s.
Note that Four Rooms involves sexual content and profanity.
