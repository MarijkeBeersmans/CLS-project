Someone to Watch Over Me
The mood of Someone to Watch Over Me is bittersweet, gloomy, and stylized.
The plot centers around witnessing a crime, a woman in danger, and law enforcement.
It is a thriller, romance, and crime movie.
Stylistically, Someone to Watch Over Me is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in an urban environment.
Someone to Watch Over Me is set in New York.
It happens in the 1980s.
The movie is known for being critically acclaimed.
Note that Someone to Watch Over Me includes brief nudity, mild violent content, and profanity.
