Wonder Boys
Wonder Boys can be described as clever, contemplative, and touching.
The plot revolves around a midlife crisis, the life of a writer, and teachers and students.
The main genres are drama and comedy.
In terms of style, Wonder Boys is talky.
In approach, it is realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Wonder Boys is set, at least in part, in the suburbs.
It is located in Pittsburgh.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The soundtrack of Wonder Boys is rock.
It is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an Oscar winner.
Note that Wonder Boys involves drugs/alcohol, sexual content, and profanity.
