Broken Embraces
The mood of Broken Embraces is clever, emotional, and stylized.
The plot centers around a destructive relationship, a life turned upside-down, and filmmaking.
It is a drama, foreign, and thriller movie.
Stylistically, Broken Embraces is postmodernist, is a film in a film, and is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The setting is Madrid.
Broken Embraces happens in the 1990s and in contemporary times.
The movie is known for being critically acclaimed.
Note that it includes nudity, sexual content, and profanity.
