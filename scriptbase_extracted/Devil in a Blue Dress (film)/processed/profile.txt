Devil in a Blue Dress
Devil in a Blue Dress can be described as clever, suspenseful, and bleak.
The plot revolves around a missing person, amateur detectives, and an obsessive quest.
The main genres are thriller, crime, and mystery.
In terms of style, Devil in a Blue Dress is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
Devil in a Blue Dress is located in Los Angeles.
It takes place in the 20th century.
The soundtrack is oldies and jazz.
Devil in a Blue Dress is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves profanity and violent content.
