Bad Lieutenant
Bad Lieutenant can be described as rough, bleak, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around going against the rules, going straight, and redemption.
The main genres are drama, independent, and crime.
In terms of style, Bad Lieutenant is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
Bad Lieutenant is located in New York.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
Bad Lieutenant is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves brief nudity, drugs/alcohol, and sexual content.
