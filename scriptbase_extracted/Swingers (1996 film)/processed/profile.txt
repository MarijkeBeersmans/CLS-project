Swingers
Swingers can be described as clever, witty, and feel good.
The plot revolves around wooing, starting over, and twentysomething life.
The main genres are comedy and independent.
In terms of style, Swingers is talky.
In approach, it is realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
Swingers is located in Las Vegas and Los Angeles.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Swingers is well suited for teens.
Note that it involves sexual content and profanity.
