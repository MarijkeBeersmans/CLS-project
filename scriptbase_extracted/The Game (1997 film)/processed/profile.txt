The Game
The Game can be described as mind bending, stylized, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a life turned upside-down, mind games, and psychological motives.
The main genres are independent, thriller, and mystery.
In terms of style, The Game is neo-noir and involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
The Game is located in San Francisco.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being a modern classic and critically acclaimed.
Note that The Game involves mild violent content.
