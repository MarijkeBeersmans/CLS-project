Lost Highway
The mood of Lost Highway is atmospheric, mind bending, and sexual.
The plot centers around voyeurism, mistaken identities, and a dangerous attraction.
It is a drama and mystery movie.
Stylistically, Lost Highway is nonlinear, is neo-noir, and is experimental.
In approach, it is serious.
It takes place, at least partly, in an urban environment.
Lost Highway happens in the 1990s.
Note that it includes violent content.
