True Grit
True Grit can be described as tense, stylized, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around tracking someone down, young heroes, and partners.
The main genres are western, drama, and adventure.
In terms of style, True Grit has a road movie structure and includes a voice over.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
True Grit is located in Oklahoma and Texas.
It takes place in the Old West and in the 19th century.
It is a remake and based on a book.
True Grit has received attention for being a blockbuster, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves violent content.
