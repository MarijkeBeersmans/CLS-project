Carrie
Carrie can be described as tense, atmospheric, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around pranks, social misfits, and high school life.
The main genres are independent, horror, and drama.
In terms of style, Carrie is gory.
In approach, it is serious.
It is set, at least in part, at a high school.
Carrie is located in the USA.
It takes place in the 20th century.
It is based on a book.
Carrie has received attention for being a classic, essential viewing, and critically acclaimed.
It is well suited for teens.
Note that it involves violent content.
