Isle of the Dead
Isle of the Dead can be described as scary, suspenseful, and tense.
The plot revolves around a plague, vampires, and curses, spells and rituals.
The main genres are horror and thriller.
In terms of style, Isle of the Dead is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, on an island.
Isle of the Dead is located in Greece.
It takes place in the 1910s.
Visually, it is black and white.
Isle of the Dead has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
