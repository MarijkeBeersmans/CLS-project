Election
Election can be described as biting, cynical, and clever.
The plot revolves around campaign, teachers and students, and high school life.
Its comic aspect comes from satire and irreverent humor.
Election's main genres are drama, comedy, and independent.
In approach, it is realistic.
It is set, at least in part, at a high school.
Election is located in Nebraska and New York.
It takes place in the 1990s.
It is based on a book.
Election has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Note that it involves sexual content and profanity.
