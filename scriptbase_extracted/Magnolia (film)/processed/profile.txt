Magnolia
Magnolia can be described as clever, bleak, and tense.
The plot revolves around a supportive spouse, redemption, and a dysfunctional family.
The main genre is drama.
In terms of style, Magnolia involves multiple stories, is episodic, and includes a voice over.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
Magnolia takes place in the 1990s.
The soundtrack is pop and rock.
The movie has received attention for being a Berlin festival winner and critically acclaimed.
