Brick
The mood of Brick is suspenseful, clever, and witty.
The plot centers around high school life, a whodunit, and an investigation.
It is an independent, mystery, and thriller movie.
Stylistically, Brick is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, at a high school.
Brick is set in California.
It happens in contemporary times.
The movie is known for being a Sundance Festival winner and critically acclaimed.
Note that Brick includes drugs/alcohol and violent content.
