The Haunting
The mood of The Haunting is scary, atmospheric, and suspenseful.
The plot centers around domestic disturbances, ghosts, and supernatural occurences.
It is a horror, thriller, and mystery movie.
Stylistically, The Haunting stars an ensemble cast.
In approach, it is serious.
It takes place, at least partly, in a haunted house and on an estate.
The Haunting is set in the USA.
It happens in the 1990s.
The musical score is orchestral.
The Haunting is a remake and based on a book.
