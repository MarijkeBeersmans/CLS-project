Dumb and Dumber
Dumb and Dumber can be described as humorous.
The plot revolves around misfit partners, an odd couple, and a goofy hero.
Its comic aspect comes from grossout humor and slapstick humor.
Dumb and Dumber's main genres are comedy and independent.
In terms of style, it has a Hollywood tone and has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is not serious and realistic.
Dumb and Dumber is located in Nebraska.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being a blockbuster and an award winner.
Dumb and Dumber is well suited for teens.
Note that it involves mild violent content.
