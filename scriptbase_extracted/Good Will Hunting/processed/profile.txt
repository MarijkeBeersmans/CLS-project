Good Will Hunting
Good Will Hunting can be described as uplifting, feel good, and captivating.
The plot revolves around a dedicated educator, a prodigy, and doctors and patients.
The main genres are drama and independent.
In terms of style, Good Will Hunting has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, at a university.
Good Will Hunting is located in Boston.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being a modern classic, an Oscar winner, and a blockbuster.
Good Will Hunting is well suited for a date night.
