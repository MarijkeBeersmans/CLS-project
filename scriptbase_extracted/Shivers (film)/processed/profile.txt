Shivers
The mood of Shivers is witty, offbeat, and scary.
The plot centers around a plague, a mad scientist, and a violence spree.
It features satire.
Shivers is an independent, horror, and sci-fi movie.
Stylistically, it is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
Shivers takes place, at least partly, on an island.
The setting is Canada.
It happens in the 20th century.
Shivers is known for being critically acclaimed.
Note that it includes nudity.
