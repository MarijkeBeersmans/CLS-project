The Wrestler
The Wrestler can be described as bleak, gloomy, and sincere.
The plot revolves around aging, a midlife crisis, and estrangement.
The main genres are drama, independent, and sports.
In approach, The Wrestler is serious and realistic.
It is located in New Jersey.
It takes place in contemporary times.
The Wrestler has received attention for being a Venice Festival winner, a Golden Globe winner, and critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol, nudity, and sexual content.
