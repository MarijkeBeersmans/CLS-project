Wag the Dog
Wag the Dog can be described as cynical, clever, and witty.
The plot revolves around a political conspiracy, media, and politics.
Its comic aspect comes from satire.
Wag the Dog's main genres are comedy, independent, and drama.
In terms of style, it is talky and features an all-star cast.
In approach, it is realistic.
Wag the Dog is located in the USA.
It takes place in the 1990s.
It is based on a book.
Wag the Dog has received attention for being a Berlin festival winner and critically acclaimed.
