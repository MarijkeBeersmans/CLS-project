The Book of Eli
The Book of Eli can be described as bleak, rough, and tense.
The plot revolves around an apocalypse, a future dystopia, and lone wolves.
The main genres are action and sci-fi.
In terms of style, The Book of Eli has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is located in the USA.
The Book of Eli is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves violent content.
