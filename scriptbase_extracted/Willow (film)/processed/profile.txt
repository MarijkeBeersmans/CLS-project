Willow
The mood of Willow is offbeat, exciting, and stylized.
The plot centers around a child in danger, an imaginary kingdom, and curses, spells and rituals.
It is an adventure and fantasy movie.
Stylistically, Willow is fairytale-like and is epic.
In approach, it is fantastical.
It is especially suggested for a family outing.
Note that Willow includes mild violent content.
