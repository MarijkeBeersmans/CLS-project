Being Human
Being Human can be described as sentimental.
The plot revolves around custody issues, samurai, and a midlife crisis.
The main genre is drama.
In terms of style, Being Human is episodic and is a melodrama.
In approach, it is serious.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Being Human is located in the Roman Empire and Rome.
It takes place in prehistorical times, in the 17th century, and in the middle ages.
