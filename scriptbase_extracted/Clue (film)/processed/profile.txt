Clue
Clue can be described as witty and mind bending.
The plot revolves around an investigation, murder, and rivalry.
Its comic aspect comes from silly humor and screwball humor.
Clue's main genres are comedy, mystery, and thriller.
In terms of style, it stars an ensemble cast.
In approach, it is not serious and realistic.
Clue is set, at least in part, indoors.
It takes place in the 1950s.
