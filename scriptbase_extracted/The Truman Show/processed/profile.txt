The Truman Show
The Truman Show can be described as clever, biting, and thought provoking.
The plot revolves around voyeurism, a TV show, and media satire.
Its comic aspect comes from satire.
The Truman Show's main genres are drama and comedy.
In terms of style, it is postmodernist and is a film in a film.
It is set, at least in part, in the suburbs.
The Truman Show is located in Hollywood.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The soundtrack is classical.
The Truman Show has received attention for being a modern classic, a Golden Globe winner, and essential viewing.
