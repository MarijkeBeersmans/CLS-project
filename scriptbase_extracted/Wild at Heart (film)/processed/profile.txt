Wild at Heart
Wild at Heart can be described as stylized, atmospheric, and offbeat.
The plot revolves around lovers on the run, fugitives, and chases.
The main genres are drama, independent, and thriller.
In terms of style, Wild at Heart is surreal, has a road movie structure, and is gory.
It is located in Texas and California.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The soundtrack of Wild at Heart is oldies and jazz.
It is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being a Cannes festival winner and a cult favorite.
Note that Wild at Heart involves strong sexual content.
