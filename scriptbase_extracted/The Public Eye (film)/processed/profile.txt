The Public Eye
The Public Eye can be described as suspenseful, tense, and rough.
The plot revolves around gangsters, cons and scams, and law enforcement.
The main genres are drama, thriller, and crime.
In approach, The Public Eye is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, at a nightclub.
It is located in New York.
The Public Eye takes place in the 20th century.
