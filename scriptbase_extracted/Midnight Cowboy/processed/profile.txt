Midnight Cowboy
Midnight Cowboy can be described as captivating, disturbing, and bittersweet.
The plot revolves around a prostitute, social misfits, and human nature.
The main genre is drama.
In approach, Midnight Cowboy is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
It is located in New York.
Midnight Cowboy takes place in the 1950s.
It is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being a classic, controversial, and an Oscar winner.
