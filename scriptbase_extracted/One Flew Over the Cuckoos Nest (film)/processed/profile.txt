One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest
The mood of One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest is disturbing, contemplative, and emotional.
The plot centers around a character who has been wrongfully committed, doctors and patients, and antiheroes.
It is a drama movie.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in a mental institution.
It happens in the 1960s.
It is based on a book.
The movie is known for being a masterpiece, a classic, and an Oscar winner.
Note that it includes brief nudity and mild violent content.
