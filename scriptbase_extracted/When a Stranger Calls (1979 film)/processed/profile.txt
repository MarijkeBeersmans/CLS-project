When a Stranger Calls
When a Stranger Calls can be described as scary, suspenseful, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around stalking, a family in danger, and mind games.
The main genres are horror and thriller.
In terms of style, When a Stranger Calls is gory.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a mental institution.
When a Stranger Calls is located in the USA.
It takes place in the 20th century.
