Amadeus
Amadeus can be described as stylized, captivating, and witty.
The plot revolves around blind ambition, a prodigy, and the desire for fame.
The main genres are drama, independent, and music-oriented.
In approach, Amadeus is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, at a royal court.
It is located in Austria.
Amadeus takes place in the 18th century.
The soundtrack is classical.
It is adapted from a play and originally a true story.
Amadeus has received attention for being a modern classic, an Oscar winner, and critically acclaimed.
