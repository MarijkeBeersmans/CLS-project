Bonnie and Clyde
Bonnie and Clyde can be described as captivating, rough, and disturbing.
The plot revolves around a murderous pair, lovers on the run, and a violence spree.
The main genres are crime, action, and romance.
In terms of style, Bonnie and Clyde has a road movie structure and stars a strong female character.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in the USA.
Bonnie and Clyde takes place in the 1930s.
It is originally a true story.
The movie has received attention for being groundbreaking, a masterpiece, and a classic.
Note that Bonnie and Clyde involves profanity.
