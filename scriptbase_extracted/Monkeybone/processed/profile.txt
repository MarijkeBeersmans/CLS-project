Monkeybone
Monkeybone can be described as stylized, offbeat, and humorous.
The plot revolves around a parallel world, imaginary themes, and disorder.
The main genres are comedy, fantasy, and animation.
In terms of style, Monkeybone is surreal.
In approach, it is not serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a hospital.
Monkeybone is based on a book.
It is well suited for a date night, a family outing, and teens.
