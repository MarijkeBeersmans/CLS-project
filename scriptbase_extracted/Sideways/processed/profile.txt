Sideways
Sideways can be described as bittersweet, clever, and biting.
The plot revolves around themes of unfulfillment, friendship, and way of life.
The main genres are drama and comedy.
In terms of style, Sideways is episodic, has a road movie structure, and is talky.
In approach, it is realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Sideways is set, at least in part, in the countryside.
It is located in California.
It takes place in contemporary times.
Sideways has received attention for being an Oscar winner and critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves strong sexual content, nudity, and profanity.
