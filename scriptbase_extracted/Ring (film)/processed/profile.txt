Ringu
Ringu can be described as scary, suspenseful, and tense.
The plot revolves around a family in danger, ghosts, and obsession.
The main genres are foreign, thriller, and horror.
In terms of style, Ringu is surreal.
In approach, it is serious.
It is located in Japan.
Ringu takes place in the 1990s.
It is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Ringu is well suited for teens.
Note that it involves mild violent content.
