Supergirl
Supergirl can be described as stylized and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around secret identities, superhuman powers, and superheroes.
The main genres are adventure, fantasy, and action.
In approach, Supergirl is fantastical and serious.
It takes place in the 1980s.
It is adapted from a comic.
Supergirl is well suited for pre-teens, a family outing, and teens.
