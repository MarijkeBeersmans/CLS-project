Shrek the Third
The mood of Shrek the Third is humorous and feel good.
The plot centers around curses, spells and rituals, a rescue, and misfits.
It features silly humor.
Shrek the Third is a comedy, animation, and adventure movie.
Stylistically, it is fairytale-like, has a Hollywood tone, and features an all-star cast.
In approach, it is not serious and fantastical.
Shrek the Third takes place, at least partly, at a royal court.
Visually, it is computer animated.
The musical score is pop.
Shrek the Third is known for being a blockbuster and an award winner.
It is especially suggested for a date night and a family outing.
