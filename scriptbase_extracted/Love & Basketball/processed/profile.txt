Love and Basketball
Love and Basketball can be described as uplifting, touching, and bittersweet.
The plot revolves around lovers reunited, teaching the ways of the world, and true love.
The main genres are drama, independent, and romance.
In approach, Love and Basketball is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
It is located in Los Angeles.
Love and Basketball takes place in the 1980s.
The soundtrack is soul and R&B.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Love and Basketball is well suited for teens.
Note that it involves profanity.
