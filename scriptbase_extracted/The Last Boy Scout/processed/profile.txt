The Last Boy Scout
The Last Boy Scout can be described as cynical, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around reluctant partners, a private detective, and murder.
The main genres are thriller, action, and crime.
In approach, The Last Boy Scout is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is fast paced.
It is located in Los Angeles.
The Last Boy Scout takes place in the 1990s.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol, nudity, and profanity.
