Star Trek
Star Trek can be described as exciting, stylized, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around space wars, destiny, and space and aliens.
The main genres are adventure, sci-fi, and action.
In terms of style, Star Trek has a Hollywood tone and is futuristic.
In approach, it is fantastical.
It is set, at least in part, on a spacecraft.
Visually, Star Trek involves special effects.
It is based on a TV series.
The movie has received attention for being a blockbuster, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
Star Trek is well suited for teens and a boys' night.
Note that it involves mild violent content.
