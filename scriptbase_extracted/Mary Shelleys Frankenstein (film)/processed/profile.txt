Mary Shelley's Frankenstein
Mary Shelley's Frankenstein can be described as atmospheric, scary, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around reincarnation, a mad scientist, and an experiment gone awry.
The main genres are horror and fantasy.
In terms of style, Mary Shelley's Frankenstein features an all-star cast, is epic, and is gothic.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It takes place in the 19th century.
Mary Shelley's Frankenstein is based on a book.
