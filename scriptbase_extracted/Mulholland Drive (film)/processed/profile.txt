Mulholland Dr.
Mulholland Dr. can be described as mind bending, stylized, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around memory loss, a mysterious character, and manipulation.
The main genres are drama, independent, and mystery.
In terms of style, Mulholland Dr. is postmodernist.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in Hollywood.
Mulholland Dr. takes place in contemporary times.
The movie has received attention for being a Cannes festival winner and critically acclaimed.
