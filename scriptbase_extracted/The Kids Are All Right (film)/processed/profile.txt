The Kids Are All Right

Stylistically, The Kids Are All Right stars a strong female character.
In approach, it is realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in the suburbs.
The Kids Are All Right is set in Los Angeles.
It happens in contemporary times.
The movie is known for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
