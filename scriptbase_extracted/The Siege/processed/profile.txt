The Siege
The Siege can be described as bleak, rough, and thought provoking.
The plot revolves around reluctant partners, special agents, and working undercover.
The main genres are thriller and action.
In terms of style, The Siege features an all-star cast and involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in New York.
The Siege takes place in the 1990s.
Note that it involves violent content.
