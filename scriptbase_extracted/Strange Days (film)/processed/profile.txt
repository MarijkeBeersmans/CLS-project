Strange Days
Strange Days can be described as bleak, exciting, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a future dystopia, chaos, and uncovering the truth.
The main genres are thriller, sci-fi, and crime.
In approach, Strange Days is serious.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
It is located in Los Angeles.
Strange Days takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being an award winner.
Note that it involves strong sexual content and profanity.
