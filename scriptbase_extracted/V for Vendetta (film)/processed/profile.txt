V For Vendetta
The mood of V For Vendetta is stylized, bleak, and captivating.
The plot centers around a revolution, a mysterious character, and a future dystopia.
It is a thriller, action, and sci-fi movie.
Stylistically, V For Vendetta is neo-noir, has a Hollywood tone, and includes a voice over.
In approach, it is serious.
It takes place, at least partly, in an urban environment.
V For Vendetta is set in London.
It is adapted from a comic.
The movie is known for being a blockbuster, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
V For Vendetta is especially suggested for teens.
Note that it includes sexual content, profanity, and violent content.
