Airplane!
The mood of Airplane! is exciting.
The plot centers around a goofy hero, saving the day, and a rescue.
It features silly humor.
Airplane! is a comedy and parody movie.
Stylistically, it has a Hollywood tone and stars an ensemble cast.
In approach, it is not serious.
Airplane! takes place, at least partly, on an airplane.
It happens in the 20th century.
The movie is known for being a modern classic, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
Airplane! is especially suggested for teens.
