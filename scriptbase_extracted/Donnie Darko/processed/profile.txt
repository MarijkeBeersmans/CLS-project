Donnie Darko
The mood of Donnie Darko is tense, atmospheric, and offbeat.
The plot centers around suburban life, teenage love, and social misfits.
It features satire.
Donnie Darko is a drama, independent, and fantasy movie.
Stylistically, it is postmodernist and is surreal.
In approach, it is serious.
Donnie Darko takes place, at least partly, in the suburbs.
The setting is Virginia.
It happens in the 1980s.
The musical score of Donnie Darko is pop, alternative, and rock.
The movie is known for being a cult favorite, essential viewing, and critically acclaimed.
It is especially suggested for teens.
Note that Donnie Darko includes profanity and violent content.
