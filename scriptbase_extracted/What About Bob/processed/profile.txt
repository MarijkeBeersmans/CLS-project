What About Bob?
The mood of What About Bob? is humorous and clever.
The plot centers around mental illness, doctors and patients, and a goofy hero.
It features farce.
What About Bob? is a comedy movie.
Stylistically, it is talky.
In approach, it is not serious and realistic.
What About Bob? is set in New Hampshire.
It happens in the 1990s.
The movie is known for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
