The Jackie Robinson Story
The Jackie Robinson Story can be described as uplifting and sincere.
The plot revolves around a sports team, racism, and going against the odds.
The main genres are drama and sports.
In terms of style, The Jackie Robinson Story is episodic.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, at a college.
The Jackie Robinson Story is located in California and New York.
It takes place in the 20th century.
Visually, it is black and white.
The Jackie Robinson Story is originally a true story.
