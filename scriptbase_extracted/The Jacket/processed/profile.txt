The Jacket
The Jacket can be described as stylized, atmospheric, and mind bending.
The plot revolves around destiny, time travel, and mental instability.
The main genres are drama, thriller, and sci-fi.
In terms of style, The Jacket is surreal.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a mental institution.
The Jacket takes place in the 1990s and in contemporary times.
The soundtrack is electronica.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that The Jacket involves violent content.
