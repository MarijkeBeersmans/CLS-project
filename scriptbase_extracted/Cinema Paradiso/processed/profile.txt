Cinema Paradiso
The mood of Cinema Paradiso is uplifting, bittersweet, and contemplative.
The plot centers around coming of age, friendship, and youth.
It is a drama and foreign movie.
Stylistically, Cinema Paradiso is a melodrama.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The pacing is slow.
Cinema Paradiso takes place, at least partly, in a village.
The setting is Sicily.
It happens in the 20th century.
Cinema Paradiso is known for being a modern classic, a Cannes festival winner, and an Oscar winner.
It is especially suggested for a date night.
