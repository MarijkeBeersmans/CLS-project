Cool Hand Luke
Cool Hand Luke can be described as captivating, thought provoking, and rough.
The plot revolves around a prison breakout, prison life, and rebelliousness.
The main genre is drama.
In approach, Cool Hand Luke is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a prison.
It takes place in the 1960s.
Cool Hand Luke is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an Oscar winner.
