Les Misérables
Les Misérables can be described as emotional, captivating, and bittersweet.
The plot revolves around going straight, redemption, and an obsessive quest.
The main genres are drama and musical.
In terms of style, Les Misérables features an all-star cast, has a Hollywood tone, and stars an ensemble cast.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in France.
Les Misérables takes place in the 19th century.
The soundtrack is show tunes.
It is adapted from a play and based on a book.
Les Misérables has received attention for being a blockbuster, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
