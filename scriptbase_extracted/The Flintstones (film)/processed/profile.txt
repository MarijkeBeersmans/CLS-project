The Flintstones
The Flintstones can be described as feel good, humorous, and offbeat.
The plot revolves around bosses and employees, an eccentric family, and workplace situations.
Its comic aspect comes from silly humor.
The Flintstones's main genres are comedy and family.
In terms of style, it features an all-star cast and has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is not serious and fantastical.
The Flintstones takes place in prehistorical times.
It is based on a TV series.
The movie has received attention for being a blockbuster and an award winner.
The Flintstones is well suited for a family outing.
