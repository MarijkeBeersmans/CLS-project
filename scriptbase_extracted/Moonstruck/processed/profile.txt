Moonstruck
Moonstruck can be described as touching, clever, and offbeat.

In approach, it is realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
Moonstruck is located in New York.
It takes place in the 1980s.
The movie has received attention for being an Oscar winner and critically acclaimed.
