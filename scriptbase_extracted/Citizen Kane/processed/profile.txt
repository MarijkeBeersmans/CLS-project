Citizen Kane
Citizen Kane can be described as clever, gloomy, and stylized.
The plot revolves around a rise to the top, media, and human nature.
The main genres are drama and period.
In terms of style, Citizen Kane is nonlinear, is a film in a film, and is episodic.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Citizen Kane is located in the USA.
It takes place in the 1920s and in the 1930s.
Visually, it is black and white.
Citizen Kane has received attention for being groundbreaking, original, and a masterpiece.
