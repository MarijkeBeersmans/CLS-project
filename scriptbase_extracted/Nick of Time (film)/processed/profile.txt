Nick of Time
The mood of Nick of Time is tense, suspenseful, and scary.
The plot centers around a missing person, kidnapping, and a race against time.
It is a thriller and drama movie.
Stylistically, Nick of Time is neo-noir and involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The setting is Los Angeles.
Nick of Time happens in the 1990s.
Note that it includes violent content.
