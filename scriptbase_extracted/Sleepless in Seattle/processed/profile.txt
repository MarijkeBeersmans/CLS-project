Sleepless in Seattle
Sleepless in Seattle can be described as feel good, sentimental, and touching.
The plot revolves around an unlikely couple, a single parent, and looking for love.
The main genres are comedy and romance.
In terms of style, Sleepless in Seattle has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
Sleepless in Seattle is located in Seattle.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being a blockbuster, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
Sleepless in Seattle is well suited for a date night and a family outing.
