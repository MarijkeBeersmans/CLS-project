The Rocky Horror Picture Show
The mood of The Rocky Horror Picture Show is offbeat, scary, and exciting.
The plot centers around transvestism, masters and servants, and a mad scientist.
It features silly humor.
The Rocky Horror Picture Show is a comedy, musical, and sci-fi movie.
Stylistically, it is gothic.
In approach, it is not serious and fantastical.
The Rocky Horror Picture Show takes place, at least partly, in a palace, castle, or temple.
It happens in the 20th century.
The musical score is show tunes.
The Rocky Horror Picture Show is adapted from a play.
The movie is known for being groundbreaking, a cult favorite, and critically acclaimed.
Note that it includes brief nudity and profanity.
