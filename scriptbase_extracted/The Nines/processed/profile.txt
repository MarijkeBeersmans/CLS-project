The Nines
The Nines can be described as contemplative, atmospheric, and thought provoking.
The plot revolves around destiny, the life of an actor, and obsession.
The main genres are drama, independent, and mystery.
In terms of style, The Nines is episodic.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is located in Los Angeles and New York.
The Nines takes place in contemporary times.
