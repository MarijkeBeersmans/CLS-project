Memento
Memento can be described as mind bending, clever, and bleak.
The plot revolves around memory loss, mistaken identities, and manipulation.
The main genres are independent, thriller, and mystery.
In terms of style, Memento is nonlinear, is neo-noir, and involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in the USA.
Memento takes place in the 1990s.
Visually, it is partly black and white.
It is derived from a short story.
Memento has received attention for being original, a modern classic, and a Sundance Festival winner.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol, profanity, and violent content.
