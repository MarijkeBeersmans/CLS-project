Requiem for a Dream
Requiem for a Dream can be described as disturbing, bleak, and tense.
The plot revolves around a downfall, drug abuse, and a destructive relationship.
The main genres are drama and period.
In terms of style, Requiem for a Dream is surreal.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
Requiem for a Dream is located in New York.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being a modern classic, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
Note that Requiem for a Dream involves drugs/alcohol and violent content.
