Smokin' Aces
Smokin' Aces can be described as rough, exciting, and stylized.
The plot revolves around gangsters, criminal heroes, and law enforcement.
Its comic aspect comes from dark humor.
Smokin' Aces's main genres are action, crime, and comedy.
In terms of style, it stars an ensemble cast and is gory.
In approach, it is realistic.
Smokin' Aces is set, at least in part, in a hotel.
It is located in Nevada.
It takes place in the 1950s.
Smokin' Aces has received attention for being an award winner.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol, nudity, and sexual content.
