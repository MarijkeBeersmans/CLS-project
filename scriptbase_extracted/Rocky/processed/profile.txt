Rocky

Stylistically, Rocky has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The setting is Philadelphia.
Rocky happens in the 20th century.
The movie is known for being a classic, an Oscar winner, and a blockbuster.
It is especially suggested for teens, a boys' night, and a family outing.
