Light Sleeper
The mood of Light Sleeper is gloomy, suspenseful, and captivating.
The plot centers around psychological motives, crimes, and themes of mind and soul.
It is a drama movie.
In approach, Light Sleeper is serious and realistic.
The setting is New York.
It happens in the 1990s.
Note that Light Sleeper includes brief nudity, mild violent content, and profanity.
