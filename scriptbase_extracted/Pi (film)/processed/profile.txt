Pi
Pi can be described as mind bending, contemplative, and atmospheric.
The plot revolves around a losing-it hero, a prodigy, and an obsessive quest.
The main genres are independent and thriller.
In terms of style, Pi is experimental, is surreal, and includes a voice over.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in New York.
Pi takes place in the 1990s.
Visually, it is black and white.
The soundtrack is electronica.
Pi has received attention for being a Sundance Festival winner, essential viewing, and critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves profanity.
