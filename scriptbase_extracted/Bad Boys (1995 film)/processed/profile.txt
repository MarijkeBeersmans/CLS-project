Bad Boys
Bad Boys can be described as exciting, stylized, and rough.
The plot revolves around witnessing a crime, working undercover, and partners against crime.
The main genres are action, comedy, and thriller.
In terms of style, Bad Boys has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is realistic.
It is located in Miami.
Bad Boys takes place in the 1990s.
It is well suited for teens and a boys' night.
Note that it involves brief nudity, profanity, and violent content.
