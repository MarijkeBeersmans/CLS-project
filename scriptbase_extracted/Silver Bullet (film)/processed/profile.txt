Silver Bullet
Silver Bullet can be described as scary, suspenseful, and tense.
The plot revolves around social relations, themes of life is a bitch, and heroes.
The main genres are horror, drama, and thriller.
In approach, Silver Bullet is serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a small town.
It takes place in the 1980s.
Silver Bullet is based on a book.
