Buffy the Vampire Slayer
Buffy the Vampire Slayer can be described as humorous, offbeat, and scary.
The plot revolves around vampires, a heroic mission, and teenage life.
Its comic aspect comes from satire.
Buffy the Vampire Slayer's main genres are comedy and horror.
In approach, it is fantastical.
It is set, at least in part, at a school.
Buffy the Vampire Slayer takes place in the 1990s.
It is well suited for teens.
