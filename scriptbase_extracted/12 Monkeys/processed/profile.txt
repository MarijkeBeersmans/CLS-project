12 Monkeys
12 Monkeys can be described as mind bending, bleak, and stylized.
The plot revolves around changing the past or future, memory loss, and a plague.
The main genres are thriller, sci-fi, and mystery.
In terms of style, 12 Monkeys is postmodernist, is nonlinear, and is futuristic.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is located in Baltimore and Philadelphia.
12 Monkeys takes place in the 1990s.
It is based on a short film and a remake.
The movie has received attention for being original, a modern classic, and critically acclaimed.
Note that 12 Monkeys involves profanity and violent content.
