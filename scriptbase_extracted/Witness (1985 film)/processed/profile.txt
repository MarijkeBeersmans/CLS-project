Witness
Witness can be described as captivating, suspenseful, and thought provoking.
The plot revolves around a "fish out of water", a police investigation, and falling in love.
The main genres are drama, thriller, and romance.
In terms of style, Witness is a melodrama.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in the countryside.
Witness is located in Philadelphia.
It takes place in the 1980s.
The movie has received attention for being an Oscar winner and critically acclaimed.
Note that Witness involves brief nudity and violent content.
