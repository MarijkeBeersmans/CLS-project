Hard to Kill
The mood of Hard to Kill is rough, suspenseful, and exciting.
The plot centers around a tough law enforcer, a police investigation, and vengeance.
It is a thriller, action, and crime movie.
In approach, Hard to Kill is serious and realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in a hospital.
It happens in the 1980s.
Hard to Kill is especially suggested for a boys' night.
