Friday the 13th
The mood of Friday the 13th is scary, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot centers around a slasher, a whodunit, and a serial killer.
It is an independent and horror movie.
Stylistically, Friday the 13th has a Hollywood tone, involves twists and turns, and is gory.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The setting is the USA.
Friday the 13th happens in the 20th century.
The movie is known for being a cult favorite and a modern classic.
It is especially suggested for teens.
Note that Friday the 13th includes strong sexual content and nudity.
