Thelma & Louise
Thelma & Louise can be described as captivating, uplifting, and bittersweet.
The plot revolves around feminism, rebelliousness, and fugitives.
The main genre is drama.
In terms of style, Thelma & Louise has a Hollywood tone and has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is located in Arkansas and Arizona.
Thelma & Louise takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being a cult favorite, a modern classic, and an Oscar winner.
It is well suited for a girls' night.
Note that Thelma & Louise involves profanity and violent content.
