The Next Three Days
The mood of The Next Three Days is bleak, gloomy, and suspenseful.
The plot centers around a prison breakout, a false accusation, and a life turned upside-down.
It is a thriller, drama, and crime movie.
In approach, The Next Three Days is serious and realistic.
The setting is Pittsburgh.
It happens in contemporary times.
The Next Three Days is inspired by a film and a remake.
Note that it includes drugs/alcohol and violent content.
