Arctic Blue
Arctic Blue can be described as exciting, suspenseful, and stylized.
The plot revolves around prison life, imprisonment or confinement, and escapades.
The main genres are independent, thriller, and action.
In approach, Arctic Blue is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, on an airplane, in the wilderness, and in a prison.
It takes place in the 1990s.
Arctic Blue is well suited for a boys' night.
