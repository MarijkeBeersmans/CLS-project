Purple Rain
The mood of Purple Rain is sentimental and captivating.
The plot centers around rising to stardom, the life of a performer, and the life of a musician.
It is a drama movie.
In approach, Purple Rain is serious and realistic.
The setting is Minnesota.
It happens in the 1980s.
The musical score of Purple Rain is R&B, soul, and rock.
The movie is known for being a blockbuster, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
Note that it includes nudity, profanity, and violent content.
