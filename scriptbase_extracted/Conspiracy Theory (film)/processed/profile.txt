Conspiracy Theory
Conspiracy Theory can be described as suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a political conspiracy, a manhunt, and amateur detectives.
The main genres are thriller and drama.
In approach, Conspiracy Theory is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
It is located in New York.
Conspiracy Theory takes place in the 1990s.
Note that it involves mild violent content.
