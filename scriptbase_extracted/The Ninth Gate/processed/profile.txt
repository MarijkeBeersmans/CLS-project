The Ninth Gate
The Ninth Gate can be described as contemplative, sexy, and scary.
The plot revolves around an obsessive quest, curses, spells and rituals, and supernatural occurences.
The main genres are independent, thriller, and mystery.
In terms of style, The Ninth Gate is gothic.
In approach, it is serious.
It is located in Paris and Spain.
The Ninth Gate takes place in the 1990s.
It is based on a book.
Note that it involves mild violent content and sexual content.
