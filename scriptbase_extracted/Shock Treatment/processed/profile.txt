Shock Treatment
Shock Treatment can be described as offbeat and humorous.
The plot revolves around a TV show, media satire, and a crumbling marriage.
Its comic aspect comes from silly humor.
Shock Treatment's main genres are comedy, musical, and fantasy.
In terms of style, it is surreal.
In approach, it is not serious.
Shock Treatment is set, at least in part, in a mental institution and in a small town.
Note that it involves profanity.
