Raging Bull
The mood of Raging Bull is tense, gloomy, and stylized.
The plot centers around a rise and fall, the life of an athlete, and redemption.
It is a drama, sports, and period movie.
In approach, Raging Bull is serious and realistic.
The setting is New York.
It happens in the 1950s and in the 1960s.
Visually, Raging Bull is black and white.
It is originally a true story and based on a book.
The movie is known for being a modern classic, a masterpiece, and an Oscar winner.
Note that Raging Bull includes violent content.
