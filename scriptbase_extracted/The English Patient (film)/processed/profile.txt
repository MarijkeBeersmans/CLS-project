The English Patient
The English Patient can be described as bittersweet, gloomy, and emotional.
The plot revolves around memory loss, tragic love, and a mysterious character.
The main genres are drama, romance, and war.
In terms of style, The English Patient is epic and is a melodrama.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a convent, in a monastery, and in a hospital.
The English Patient is located in Africa and Italy.
It takes place in the 20th century.
The movie has received attention for being a modern classic, an Oscar winner, and a blockbuster.
The English Patient is well suited for a date night.
Note that it involves violent content.
