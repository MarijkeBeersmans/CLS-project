Minority Report
Minority Report can be described as stylized, suspenseful, and clever.
The plot revolves around a frame-up, fugitives, and a future dystopia.
The main genres are thriller, crime, and sci-fi.
In terms of style, Minority Report is neo-noir and has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is located in Washington DC.
Visually, Minority Report involves special effects.
It is derived from a short story.
The movie has received attention for being a blockbuster, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
Note that Minority Report involves violent content.
