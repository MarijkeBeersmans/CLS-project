The Lost Boys
The Lost Boys can be described as stylized, suspenseful, and humorous.
The plot revolves around vampires, a dangerous attraction, and teenage life.
The main genres are horror, comedy, and thriller.
In terms of style, The Lost Boys is gory.
It is set, at least in part, in a small town.
It is located in California.
The Lost Boys takes place in the 1980s.
The soundtrack is rock.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
Note that The Lost Boys involves violent content.
