Children of Men
The mood of Children of Men is bleak, atmospheric, and captivating.
The plot centers around a future dystopia, chaos and mayhem, and antiheroes.
It is a sci-fi, drama, and thriller movie.
Stylistically, Children of Men has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is serious.
It takes place, at least partly, in an urban environment.
Children of Men is set in London.
The movie is known for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Note that it includes violent content.
