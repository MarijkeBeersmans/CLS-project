Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas
Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas can be described as biting, cynical, and captivating.
The plot revolves around drug abuse, media, and partners.
Its comic aspect comes from irreverent humor.
Its main genres are comedy and crime.
In terms of style, it is postmodernist, is surreal, and has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is realistic.
Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas is located in Las Vegas.
It takes place in the 20th century.
The soundtrack is rock.
Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being original.
Note that it involves brief nudity, drugs/alcohol, and profanity.
