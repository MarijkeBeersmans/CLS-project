The Thing Called Love
The Thing Called Love can be described as feel good, touching, and humorous.
The plot revolves around showbiz, hopes, and friendship.
The main genres are drama, comedy, and romance.
In terms of style, The Thing Called Love stars an ensemble cast and is talky.
In approach, it is realistic.
It is located in Tennessee and New York.
The Thing Called Love takes place in the 1990s.
It is well suited for a date night.
