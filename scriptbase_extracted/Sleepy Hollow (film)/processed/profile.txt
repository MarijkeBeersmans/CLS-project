Sleepy Hollow
Sleepy Hollow can be described as stylized, atmospheric, and captivating.
The plot revolves around an ancient mystery, legends and myths, and curses, spells and rituals.
The main genres are mystery and horror.
In terms of style, Sleepy Hollow is gothic, has a Hollywood tone, and is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a small town.
Sleepy Hollow takes place in the 18th century.
The soundtrack is orchestral.
It is derived from a short story.
Sleepy Hollow has received attention for being a blockbuster, critically acclaimed, and an award winner.
Note that it involves sexual content.
