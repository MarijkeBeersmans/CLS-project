Snow Falling on Cedars
Snow Falling on Cedars can be described as bittersweet, thought provoking, and contemplative.
The plot revolves around social decay, the protagonist as suspect, and racism.
The main genres are drama, romance, and period.
In terms of style, Snow Falling on Cedars is a melodrama.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Snow Falling on Cedars is set, at least in part, in a small town.
It takes place during World War 2.
The movie has received attention for being an Oscar winner.
