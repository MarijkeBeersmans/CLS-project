Life of Pi
Life of Pi can be described as stylized, captivating, and contemplative.
The plot revolves around young heroes, survival, and imprisonment or confinement.
The main genres are drama and adventure.
In terms of style, Life of Pi is epic.
In approach, it is serious.
It is set, at least in part, on a ship.
Visually, Life of Pi is 3D.
It is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being an Oscar winner, a blockbuster, and critically acclaimed.
