A Nightmare on Elm Street
A Nightmare on Elm Street can be described as scary, suspenseful, and rough.
The plot revolves around a slasher, teenage life, and deadly occurences.
The main genres are independent, horror, and thriller.
In terms of style, A Nightmare on Elm Street is surreal, involves twists and turns, and is gory.
In approach, it is serious.
It is set, at least in part, at a high school and in a small town.
A Nightmare on Elm Street is located in the USA.
It takes place in the 1980s.
The movie has received attention for being a modern classic, essential viewing, and critically acclaimed.
