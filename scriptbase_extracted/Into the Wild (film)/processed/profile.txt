Into the Wild
Into the Wild can be described as captivating, contemplative, and gloomy.
The plot revolves around an obsessive quest, way of life, and escapades.
The main genres are drama, independent, and adventure.
In terms of style, Into the Wild is episodic and has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Into the Wild is set, at least in part, in the wilderness.
It is located in Alaska.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The soundtrack of Into the Wild is alternative and rock.
It is originally a true story and based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Note that Into the Wild involves nudity and profanity.
