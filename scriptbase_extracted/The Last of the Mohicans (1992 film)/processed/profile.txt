The Last of the Mohicans
The mood of The Last of the Mohicans is emotional, captivating, and touching.
The plot centers around forbidden love, falling in love, and rivalry.
It is a romance, drama, and adventure movie.
Stylistically, The Last of the Mohicans has a Hollywood tone and is epic.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in the wilderness.
The Last of the Mohicans is set in the USA.
It happens in the 18th century.
The musical score is world music and orchestral.
The Last of the Mohicans is a remake and based on a book.
The movie is known for being critically acclaimed and an Oscar winner.
Note that it includes violent content.
