The Bonfire of the Vanities
The Bonfire of the Vanities can be described as cynical, witty, and clever.
The plot revolves around legal action, interracial relations, and legal issues.
Its comic aspect comes from dark humor and satire.
The Bonfire of the Vanities's main genres are drama and comedy.
In approach, it is realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a courtroom.
The Bonfire of the Vanities is located in New York.
It takes place in the 1980s.
It is based on a book.
