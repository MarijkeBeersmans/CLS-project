Legion
Legion can be described as suspenseful, bleak, and tense.
The plot revolves around an apocalypse, saving the world, and chaos.
The main genres are thriller, action, and fantasy.
In terms of style, Legion includes a voice over, stars an ensemble cast, and is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
The storytelling is fast paced.
Legion is set, at least in part, in a diner.
It is located in the USA.
It takes place in contemporary times.
Legion is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves profanity and violent content.
