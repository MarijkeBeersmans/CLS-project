The Shawshank Redemption
The mood of The Shawshank Redemption is contemplative, captivating, and touching.
The plot centers around prison life, a false accusation, and redemption.
It is a drama and period movie.
Stylistically, The Shawshank Redemption is talky.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It takes place, at least partly, in a prison.
The Shawshank Redemption is set in the USA.
It happens in the 1950s and in the 1960s.
It is derived from a short story.
The Shawshank Redemption is known for being a modern classic, essential viewing, and critically acclaimed.
Note that it includes mild violent content and profanity.
