Hellraiser
Hellraiser can be described as scary and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a dangerous attraction, good versus evil, and deadly occurences.
The main genre is horror.
In terms of style, Hellraiser is gothic and is gory.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It takes place in the 1980s.
Hellraiser is based on a book.
Note that it involves violent content.
