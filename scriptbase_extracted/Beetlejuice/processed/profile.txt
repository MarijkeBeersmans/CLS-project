Beetlejuice
The mood of Beetlejuice is offbeat, humorous, and biting.
The plot centers around the afterlife, ghosts, and supernatural occurences.
It features dark humor.
Beetlejuice is a comedy and fantasy movie.
Stylistically, it is gothic and is surreal.
In approach, it is fantastical.
Beetlejuice takes place, at least partly, in a haunted house and indoors.
The setting is New England.
It happens in the 1980s.
Beetlejuice is known for being a cult favorite, a blockbuster, and critically acclaimed.
It is especially suggested for a family outing and teens.
Note that it includes mild violent content.
