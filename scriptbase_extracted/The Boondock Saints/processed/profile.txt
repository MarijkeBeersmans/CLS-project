The Boondock Saints
The Boondock Saints can be described as rough, exciting, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a murderous pair, a violence spree, and vigilantism.
The main genres are independent, thriller, and crime.
In approach, The Boondock Saints is serious and realistic.
It is located in Massachusetts.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The Boondock Saints is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that it involves sexual content and profanity.
