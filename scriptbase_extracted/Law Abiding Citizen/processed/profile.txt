Law Abiding Citizen
Law Abiding Citizen can be described as tense, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around fighting the system, vengeance, and rivalry.
The main genres are thriller, drama, and crime.
In terms of style, Law Abiding Citizen involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a prison.
Law Abiding Citizen is located in Philadelphia.
It takes place in contemporary times.
Note that it involves profanity.
