Peeping Tom
Peeping Tom can be described as bleak, tense, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a psychopath, a serial killer, and psychological motives.
The main genres are thriller and horror.
In approach, Peeping Tom is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
It is located in England.
Peeping Tom takes place in the 1950s.
The movie has received attention for being a masterpiece, controversial, and critically acclaimed.
Note that it involves violent content.
