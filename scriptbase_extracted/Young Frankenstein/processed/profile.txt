Young Frankenstein
Young Frankenstein can be described as humorous and offbeat.
The plot revolves around a mad scientist, an experiment gone awry, and disorder.
Its comic aspect comes from silly humor and slapstick humor.
Young Frankenstein's main genres are comedy and parody.
In terms of style, it is gothic.
In approach, it is not serious and fantastical.
Young Frankenstein is set, at least in part, in a palace, castle, or temple.
It is located in Europe.
Visually, it is black and white.
The soundtrack of Young Frankenstein is orchestral.
It is inspired by a film and based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being a cult favorite, a blockbuster, and critically acclaimed.
