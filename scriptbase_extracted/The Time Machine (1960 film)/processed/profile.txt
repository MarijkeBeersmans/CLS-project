The Time Machine
The Time Machine can be described as atmospheric and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around changing the past or future, time travel, and a future dystopia.
The main genres are adventure, sci-fi, and fantasy.
In approach, The Time Machine is fantastical and serious.
It is located in England.
It takes place during World War 1, in the 1900s, and in the Victorian era.
The Time Machine is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being a classic.
It is well suited for a family outing.
