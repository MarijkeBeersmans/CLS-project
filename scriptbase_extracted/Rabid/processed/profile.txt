Rabid
Rabid can be described as scary, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a mutant, vampires, and chaos.
The main genres are thriller and horror.
In terms of style, Rabid is experimental and is gory.
In approach, it is serious.
It is set, at least in part, in a clinic.
Rabid is located in Canada.
It takes place in the 20th century.
