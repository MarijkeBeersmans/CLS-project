Idiocracy
The mood of Idiocracy is humorous, thought provoking, and offbeat.
The plot centers around social decay, a future dystopia, and chaos.
It features satire and grossout humor.
Idiocracy is a comedy and adventure movie.
Stylistically, it includes a voice over and is talky.
In approach, it is not serious and fantastical.
Idiocracy is set in the USA.
Note that it includes profanity.
