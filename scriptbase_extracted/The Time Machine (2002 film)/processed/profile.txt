The Time Machine
The Time Machine can be described as atmospheric and stylized.
The plot revolves around changing the past or future, time travel, and a future dystopia.
The main genres are action, adventure, and sci-fi.
In terms of style, The Time Machine has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is located in New York.
The Time Machine takes place in the 19th century.
It is a remake and based on a book.
Note that it involves violent content.
