Casablanca
Casablanca can be described as bittersweet, emotional, and captivating.
The plot revolves around lovers reunited, wartime life, and political unrest.
The main genres are drama and romance.
In terms of style, Casablanca has a Hollywood tone and is a melodrama.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
Casablanca is located in Morocco.
It takes place in the 20th century.
Visually, it is black and white.
The soundtrack of Casablanca is orchestral.
It is adapted from a play.
The movie has received attention for being a masterpiece, a classic, and an Oscar winner.
Casablanca is well suited for a date night.
