The Relic
The mood of The Relic is scary, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot centers around chaos and mayhem, deadly creatures, and a race against time.
It is a thriller, horror, and sci-fi movie.
Stylistically, The Relic is futuristic and is gory.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It takes place, at least partly, on a ship.
The Relic is set in Chicago.
It happens in the 1990s.
It is based on a book.
