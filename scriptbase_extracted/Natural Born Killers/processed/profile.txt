Natural Born Killers
Natural Born Killers can be described as biting, stylized, and offbeat.
The plot revolves around a murderous pair, lovers on the run, and media satire.
Its comic aspect comes from dark humor and satire.
Natural Born Killers's main genres are crime, drama, and action.
In terms of style, it is postmodernist, is surreal, and has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
Natural Born Killers is fast paced.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The movie has received attention for being essential viewing and an award winner.
Note that Natural Born Killers involves sexual content and profanity.
