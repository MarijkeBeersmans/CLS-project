The Leopard Man
The Leopard Man can be described as tense, scary, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around dangerous animals, a serial killer, and deadly occurences.
The main genres are thriller and mystery.
In approach, The Leopard Man is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, at a nightclub and in the countryside.
It is located in New Mexico.
Visually, The Leopard Man is black and white.
It is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
The Leopard Man is well suited for a boys' night.
