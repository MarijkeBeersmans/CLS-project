Death to Smoochy
Death to Smoochy can be described as cynical, clever, and witty.
The plot revolves around a TV show, media satire, and the life of a performer.
Its comic aspect comes from satire.
Death to Smoochy's main genre is comedy.
In terms of style, it is surreal.
In approach, it is realistic.
Death to Smoochy is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
It is located in New York.
It takes place in contemporary times.
Note that Death to Smoochy involves profanity.
