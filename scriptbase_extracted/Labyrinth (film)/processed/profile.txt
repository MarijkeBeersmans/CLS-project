Labyrinth
The mood of Labyrinth is stylized, offbeat, and feel good.
The plot centers around a wish come true, a child in danger, and an imaginary kingdom.
It is an adventure, family, and fantasy movie.
Stylistically, Labyrinth is fairytale-like and is gothic.
In approach, it is fantastical.
Visually, it involves puppetry.
The musical score of Labyrinth is rock.
The movie is known for being an award winner.
It is especially suggested for pre-teens and a family outing.
