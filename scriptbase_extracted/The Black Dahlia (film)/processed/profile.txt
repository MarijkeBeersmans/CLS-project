The Black Dahlia
The Black Dahlia can be described as gloomy, atmospheric, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a police investigation, a love triangle, and power relations.
The main genres are drama, crime, and mystery.
In terms of style, The Black Dahlia is neo-noir.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The storytelling is slow paced.
The Black Dahlia is located in Hollywood.
It takes place in the 20th century.
It is based on a book.
Note that The Black Dahlia involves sexual content and profanity.
