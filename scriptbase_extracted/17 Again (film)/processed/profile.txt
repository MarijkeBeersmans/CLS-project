17 Again
17 Again can be described as humorous and feel good.
The plot revolves around a wish come true, adolescence vs.
adulthood, and social relations.
The main genres are comedy, family, and fantasy.
In terms of style, 17 Again has a Hollywood tone.
It is set, at least in part, at a high school.
It takes place in contemporary times.
17 Again is well suited for a date night and teens.
