Timber Falls
Timber Falls can be described as scary, rough, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around isolation, kidnapping, and a slasher.
The main genres are thriller and horror.
In terms of style, Timber Falls is gory.
In approach, it is serious.
It is set, at least in part, in the mountains.
Timber Falls is located in West Virginia.
It takes place in contemporary times.
