Three Kings
Three Kings can be described as stylized, biting, and thought provoking.
The plot revolves around a moral dilemma, anti-war themes, and a treasure hunt.
Its comic aspect comes from dark humor.
Three Kings's main genres are drama, war, and comedy.
In terms of style, it is surreal.
In approach, it is realistic.
Three Kings is set, at least in part, in a battlezone and in the desert.
It is located in Iraq.
It takes place during the Gulf War.
Three Kings has received attention for being critically acclaimed and an award winner.
Note that it involves sexual content and profanity.
