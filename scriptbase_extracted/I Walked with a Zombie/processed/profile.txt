I Walked With a Zombie
I Walked With a Zombie can be described as atmospheric, scary, and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around zombies, mind games, and curses, spells and rituals.
The main genres are thriller and horror.
In approach, I Walked With a Zombie is serious.
It is set, at least in part, on an island.
It takes place in the 20th century.
Visually, I Walked With a Zombie is black and white.
It is based on a book.
The movie has received attention for being critically acclaimed.
