Clerks
The mood of Clerks is cynical, witty, and clever.
The plot centers around twentysomething life, workplace situations, and themes of unfulfillment.
It features satire.
Clerks is a comedy and independent movie.
Stylistically, it is talky.
In approach, it is realistic.
The pacing of Clerks is slow.
It takes place, at least partly, in a store.
The setting is New Jersey.
Clerks happens in the 1990s.
Visually, it is black and white.
The movie is known for being a cult favorite, a Sundance Festival winner, and essential viewing.
Clerks is especially suggested for teens.
Note that it includes profanity.
