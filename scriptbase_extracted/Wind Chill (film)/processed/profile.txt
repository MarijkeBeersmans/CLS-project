Wind Chill
Wind Chill can be described as scary and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around murder, deadly occurences, and danger.
The main genres are thriller and horror.
In terms of style, Wind Chill has a road movie structure.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, in a bank, in the wilderness, and at a college.
Wind Chill takes place in the 1950s.
