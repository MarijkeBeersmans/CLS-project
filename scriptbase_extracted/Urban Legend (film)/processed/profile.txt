Urban Legend
Urban Legend can be described as scary and suspenseful.
The plot revolves around a slasher, a whodunit, and a serial killer.
The main genres are horror, thriller, and mystery.
In terms of style, Urban Legend involves twists and turns and is gory.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
It is set, at least in part, at a college.
Urban Legend takes place in the 1990s.
It is well suited for teens and a date night.
Note that it involves profanity.
