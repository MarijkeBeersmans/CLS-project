Roughshod
The plot of Roughshod revolves around crimes, a prostitute, and being on the run.
The main genres are western and drama.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
Roughshod is set, at least in part, on a farm.
It is located in California.
It takes place in the Old West and in the 19th century.
Visually, Roughshod is black and white.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
